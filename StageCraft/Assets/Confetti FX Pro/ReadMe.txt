-------------------
Confetti FX Pro
-------------------

Confetti FX Pro is a collection of confetti particle effects that you can easily drag-n-drop into your scene! 

Designed for the following scenes: celebrations, victory, festivals, etc.

Contains:
- 16 confetti shapes. 
- 17 demo scenes.
- 1 background particle.
- 3 confetti styles: Original, Glow and Outline.
- 390 particle prefabs.

The demo scene is located in the folder "\Assets\Confetti FX Pro\Demo Scenes\".

This project has been tested on unity 2017.4.1/ 2018.4.3/ 2019.3.14.
